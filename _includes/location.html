By car:

Via the A 27, take the exit Bremerhaven Mitte, continue on the Grimsbystraße, continue on Lloydstraße, turn left into the Columbusstraße. The hotel is on the right.

Additional parking is available nearby.
If available, you are welcome to use the hotel's own parking garage
(€ 16.00 per day)

By public transport:

Stop is "Bremerhaven central station".
From there you can reach us in 10 minutes by taxi or you can use the bus lines 501, 502, 505, 506, 509.
